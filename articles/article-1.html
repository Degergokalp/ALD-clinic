FUE Hair Transplant: Benefits and Stages

March 15, 2024 Methods 8 min read

What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) is a modern hair transplantation technique that involves extracting individual hair follicles from the donor area and implanting them into the recipient area. This minimally invasive procedure has revolutionized the field of hair restoration.

Key Benefits of FUE

  • Minimal scarring compared to traditional methods
  • Natural-looking results
  • Quicker recovery time
  • Less post-operative pain
  • No linear scar in the donor area

The FUE Procedure: Step by Step

1. Consultation and Planning

During your initial consultation, our expert team will assess your hair loss pattern, discuss your expectations, and create a personalized treatment plan. We'll determine the number of grafts needed and design your new hairline.

2. Preparation

On the day of the procedure, we'll prepare the donor and recipient areas. The donor area will be trimmed short to facilitate follicle extraction.

3. Extraction Phase

Using specialized micro-punches, individual follicular units are carefully extracted from the donor area. Our surgeons use high-precision instruments to ensure minimal damage to the follicles.

4. Implantation

Tiny recipient sites are created in the balding areas, and the extracted follicles are precisely placed to ensure natural growth direction and density.

Recovery and Results

Most patients return to normal activities within a week. Initial growth typically begins within 3-4 months, with final results visible after 12-18 months.
