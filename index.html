Hair Transplant Steps

1

Area 51 Analysis 🎯

Welcome to the mission! Our first task is to analyze the "landing zones" (aka your scalp). Think of it as mapping out the perfect real estate for your new hair—prime locations only!

2

Blood Work—Science Rules! 🧪

Before we get to the fun stuff, we'll run some blood tests to make sure your body's ready for action. Don't worry, no vampires involved—just science doing its thing.

3

FUE or DHI—The Real Magic! ✨

Here comes the magic! Hair follicles are gently extracted with surgeon-level precision. It's like rescuing tiny heroes ready to save your hair game.

4

Music Time 🎵

As we're working on your hair, why not listen to some tunes? This is the fun part—maybe even prepare a playlist!

5

Kebab Time 🍢

After the procedure, it's time to celebrate with some tasty treats! You've earned it—enjoy this part of the journey.

6

Back to the Hotel 🏨

Once you've refueled and rested, we'll transfer you back to your hotel for some well-deserved relaxation. Rest up and get ready to shine!
