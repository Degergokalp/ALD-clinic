Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many grafts do I need?

The number of grafts you need depends on the extent of your hair loss and your desired results.

2. How long does a hair transplantation procedure take?

The procedure usually takes around 6 to 8 hours, depending on the grafts and technique used. We ensure a comfortable experience for all our patients. (Meal and rest breaks will be taken)

3. Are the results permanent?

Yes, the results are permanent. The transplanted hair comes from the back or sides of your scalp, which are genetically resistant to hair loss.

4. Can I resume normal activities after the procedure?

Most patients can return to light daily activities the day after the procedure, but we recommend avoiding strenuous activities and direct sunlight for at least 10 to 14 days.

5. Will the transplanted hair look natural?

Absolutely! Our advanced techniques, like FUE and DHI, ensure the transplanted hair blends seamlessly with your natural hair for a completely natural look.

6. Do I need to shave my head for the procedure?

Not always! Shaving is required for some techniques, but others, like DHI, may not require full shaving. Reach out to us today to find out the best option for you.
